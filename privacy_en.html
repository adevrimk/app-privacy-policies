At Botaniq ("we", "us", or "the app"), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the information collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

Our application may collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing plant identification services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your photos for plant identification may be processed by secure AI services.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your data. Please contact us for this.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are effective upon posting.
